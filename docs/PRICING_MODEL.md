# AstroYou Pricing Model

## Currency

- 1 AstroYou credit is the internal wallet unit.
- Credits are used for AI astrologer sessions and PDF reports.
- Purchased credits stay in the wallet until used.
- Plan credits are granted monthly.
- Consumer-facing prices are charged as shown in checkout.

## AI Astrologer Sessions

| Persona type | Price |
| --- | ---: |
| Standard guidance | 5 credits/min |
| Specialist guidance | 8 credits/min |
| Classical/premium guidance | 10 credits/min |

This keeps the visible session cost below major human astrologer marketplaces while still preserving paid intent.

## Subscriptions

| Plan | Price | Monthly credits | Max standard consult time |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Free | ₹0 | 15 | 3 min |
| Premium | ₹499/mo | 700 | 140 min |
| Pro | ₹999/mo | 1,600 | 320 min |

Premium is the daily-use plan. Pro is for heavy consultation, yearly forecasts, astrocartography, priority personas, and advanced tools.
