## Replacement Path

When real people are added, keep the same catalogue fields:

- `name`
- `title`
- `specialty`
- `bio`
- `profileIntro`
- `languages`
- `methods`
- `bestFor`
- `guidanceStyle`
- `sampleQuestions`
- `pricePerMin`

Do not add claims like verified, years of experience, degrees, review counts, or live availability unless they are real and backed by the admin/trust system.
