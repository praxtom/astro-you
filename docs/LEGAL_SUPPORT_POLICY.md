## Implemented Routes

- `/terms`
- `/privacy`
- `/refund-policy`
- `/disclaimer`
- `/support`

## Support Categories

- Refund review
- Payment issue
- Report issue
- Consultation billing
- Account or data
- Technical issue
- Other

## Refund Rules

Reviewable:

- Failed payments where credits or plan access were not delivered.
- Duplicate charges.
- Unused purchased credits.
- Report failures after credits were charged.
- Consultation billing mismatch against the server ledger.

Normally not refundable:

- Consumed credits.
- Dissatisfaction with an interpretation or prediction.
- Subscription periods where credits or paid benefits were already used.
- Decisions made from astrology or AI-generated guidance.
